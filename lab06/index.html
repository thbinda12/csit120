Website Evaluation: Towson University

Website URL

https://www.towson.edu

Website Name

Towson University

Target Audience

The website targets students who study at Towson and parents, teachers, and community members who want to learn about the university.

How the Site Is Organized

The site is well organized with a menu bar with main sections like About TU, Academics, Admissions & Aid, and Student Life. It uses large pictures and simple layouts that make it easy to find information.The main bar is easy to understand and effective.

CRAP Design Principle Example

Contrast: The black and gold colors make text stand out and easily to locate when reading.
Repetition: Fonts and colors are the same on every page.
Alignment: Everything lines up neatly in the grid.
Proximity: Related info is grouped together.

Accessibility Checker Audit Score

The site scored high on accessibility with only a few warnings for color contrast and missing alt text. Towson also lists accessibility as a core goal on their web standards page.

Site Effectiveness

The website helps users find what they need quickly. The sections and buttons like “Apply” or “Visit” are clear and easy to find.

Site Efficiency

Navigation is simple and fast. The site loads a bit slower on some pages because of large photos and videos.

Engagement

The site looks professional and is interesting to explore . The colors, videos, and pictures make it fun to look at and show Towson’s spirit.

Recommendation

They could make text a bit bigger, fix some contrast issues, and make sure all images have alt text. This would make the site even more user-friendly.
