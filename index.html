My Favorite Foods

Published on September 17, 2025 by Binda Thapa

Sushi

Sushi is a Japanese dish made with vinegared rice, fresh fish, and vegetables. It is both delicious and an art form, offering a balance of flavor and presentation.

What I Liked

  1. Freshness of ingredients
  2. Unique combinations of flavors
  3. Beautiful presentation

Pizza

Pizza is an Italian classic that has become loved all over the world. With its crispy crust, savory sauce, and melted cheese, it is the ultimate comfort food.

What I Liked

  • Cheesy goodness
  • Endless topping choices

Explore more here: Pizza Wikipedia

Chocolate Cake

Chocolate cake is a rich and indulgent dessert that never fails to satisfy. Its moist layers and sweet flavor make it a timeless favorite for celebrations.
